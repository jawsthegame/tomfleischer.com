My Two Cents

Engineering is not about doing something fancy. It is not about showing off. It is about solving problems, and solving problems alone. That being said, there is a lot of debate over the best way to a solution. I am a strong believer in iterative development. It provides enumerable benefits to both engineers and their product counterparts. I believe in absolute transparency. The engineers should know the long term direction of the product, and the product managers should know what they getting as quickly and as often as possible.
